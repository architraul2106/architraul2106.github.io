StellarXplorers

What is StellarXplorers?

StellarXplorers is a competitive yet fun competition for high school kids all around the globe to take part in. The competition consists of space-related challenges in a series of online rounds, each lasting about 6 hours. Teams of up to 6 students use programs like Excel and STK-12, a physics-based simulation of the solar system, to solve these challenges.

Scenario

The picture to the left shows one of the scenarios that was provided to us. In this scenario, we were to design orbit plans for the satellite in an attempt to access the YZ route for the longest duration during a 30-day period. To achieve this, we had to change the semi-major axis, inclination, true anomaly, and RAAN.

Achievements

  • During the first three rounds, we were ranked in the top 3 teams in the world as we had received close to the maximum possible score on each scenario.
  • From the 200 international teams, we placed in the top 15 leading us to reach the semifinal round.
  • We became the first school in school history to reach the semifinals and were announced in the school newspaper as well as the Instagram.

About Us

At The Tutorium, we are committed to empowering students by providing personalized and engaging learning experiences. Our dedicated team of experienced tutors fosters critical thinking skills, demystifies complex topics, and instills a growth mindset. Through a tailored approach and nurturing environment, we aim to equip students with a solid foundation for academic challenges and future careers in technology. Our goal is to shape confident learners and creative problem solvers, making a lasting impact on their academic success and personal growth, ensuring a brighter future.

The Classes

The first year of the workshop, we publized Tutorium through websites like Facebook, Whatsapp, and Nextdoor and got a total of 25 registrations. Due to the positive feedback from both parents and students, we decided to continue these workshops for the summer of 2023. We didn't hold back, the team continued to push forward, adding the Python programming and 4th grade math class to the mix. Also, we hired student volenteers to also aid in teaching the courses leading to a total of 10 classes rather than the 8 from the prior year.

Future Plans

Over this year we plan on expanding to involve more than 4 other schools throughout the Orange County including , Orange High, Garden Grove and Westminster. Also, to accomadate more students from around Orange County we have plans to expand to 2 more locations. These locations will be lead mostly by the new high schools. However, one of our biggest goals is to be able to be endourced by schools who have a multitude of struggling students, from this we can give specific students more attention and through contact with their teachers we hope that it would make their learning experience more compatable.

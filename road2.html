Competition

Hosting an event for 125 kids, each team of three will be given a robot kit which they will use to build the robot in a two-hour time period. Each kit was put together by the engineering team making it both fast and easy to build. Participants will also have the option to pick between the five different robot attachments - goalie, midfielder, striker, defender, speedster. The teams will be randomized for the competition with 3 robots on each team. The winner of the tournament will be provided with a scholarship as well as gifts from our sponsors.
